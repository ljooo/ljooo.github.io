not that long ago
you remember.
we sat near each other and maybe we
dressed similarly or liked the same things
and maybe we talked about books we loved or how to make art.
maybe it was sunny out and we craned our necks out the window
or walked around the park with our legs out.
at some point, though, we became sisters.
we drove through california with the windows down. we made art
with pillows and pool noodles. we ate well and rested.
at some point, you found out i was toothless
and in love.
